Rector Police Department

Located at 407 South Stewart Street, the Rector Police Department is committed to serving and protecting the citizens of Rector, Arkansas with integrity, professionalism, and dedication. Our department operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week, ensuring that help is always just a call away. 870-595-2423

Police Department Staff

Staffed by a dedicated team of men and women, the Rector Police Department works tirelessly to maintain the safety and well being of our community. Whether it is responding to emergencies, patrolling our neighborhoods, or just visiting with our neighbors in Rector, we are here to serve you.

If you need assistance or wish to speak with an Officer, please don't hesitate to contact us at 870-595-2423.

Together, we can keep Rector a safe and welcoming home for all.

- Chief Kelly Baker
